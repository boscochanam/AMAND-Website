Membership Details:

Membership of the association is open to any person from the Manipur irrespective of his/her religion, caste, colour, creed etc. who has attended an age of majority and is self employed, employed/retired either in the central Government or Maharastra Government or any other reputed private/public institutions based in/around Pune.

Member shall be bound by the Rules & Regulations of the Association. He/She should accept in writing the rule & regulation of this association.

Membership of the Association shall be granted to the eligible applicant on approval of the Managing Committee. The Association reserves the right to make an enroll new membership and also to reject any membership application without giving any reason.

Types of Memberships:


Lifetime Membership

Life membership is granted to a person whose membership application has been approved by the managing committee and has paid Rs 1000/- as one time membership fee and has agreed to subscribe Rs 100/- per month which is amendable from time to time as decided by the General Body of the association.

Regular Membership

Ordinary membership is granted to a person for a period of one year whose membership application has been approved by the managing committee and has paid Rs 200/- as yearly membership fee and Rs 50/- as admission fee and has agreed to subscribe Rs 100/- per month till the date of expiry of his/her membership, which is amendable from time to time as decided by the General Body of the association.

Honorary Membership

Any person who has rendered distinguish service to the society or who is celebrating high public esteem for excellent in his profession may be admitted as an honorary member of the Association for such periods as a managing committee may think fit and proper. Honorary member are entitle to participate in the deliberation of the association in its general body meeting, but are not eligible to vote on any matter of the association.

About Us

The Association of Manipuri Diaspora (AMAND) Pune is a non-profit, non-government community organisation founded by like minded people of Manipur residing in and around Pune. The association was founded on the 11th day of September 2011.
